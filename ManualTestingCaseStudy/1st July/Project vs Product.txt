PRODUCTS VS PROJECTS

1. PRODUCT
A product is a software application or platform developed for end users. It is continuously maintained and updated with new features and improvements.
Examples:
•	Flipkart
•	Netflix
•	Spotify
•	Whatsapp
•	Instagram
•	Youtube
•	Rapido
•	Google Maps
•	Zoom
•	Blinkit

2. PROJECT
A project is software developed to solve a specific business problem or automate a business process.
Examples:
•	Job Portal System   (LinkedIn / Naukri type)
•	Professional Networking Platform  (LinkedIn type)
•	Doctor Appointment Booking System
•	Student Management System
•	Payroll Management System
•	Online Tutoring Platform
•	Matrimonial Portal (Shaadi type)
•	Billing & Invoicing System
•	Hospital Management System
•	Banking Management System
